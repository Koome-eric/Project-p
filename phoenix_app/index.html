frequently asked queries

How can I apply for an academic writer position?

Application is easy: fill out the application form by providing basic information. After you hit the “Register” button, you will be allotted with a personal page, but it will be inactive until we review your application. Stay tuned - we will reach out to you!

I have applied and been given the required personal page, what next?

Once you have your application form submitted and have been given your personal page on StudyWriters.com, we will review your information and test papers to see whether your skills and quality meet our expectations. In case of approval, you will be contacted with further instructions - please stay tuned!

How do I pass the proficiency test, what does it incorporate?

The proficiency test is a set of several simple multiple-choice questions that intend to evaluate your basic English language command. If you are good at English, the test will take you literally just a few minutes, but it will help us to understand that we have found a potentially professional writer.

I submitted the test. How long does it take for you to handout a decision?

Usually, we make a decision per each applicant within 5-10 days after we receive an application. Sometimes, however, it might take longer due to some externalities, but we are doing our best to move fast because we understand your anxiety.
